Some projects should not be split into separate jobs.

If the fence line changes how the yard, driveway, or patio should read, it usually makes more sense to plan those elements together. That is especially true when the entry sequence, backyard finish, or overall curb appeal matter.

Bundling the work helps with alignment, transitions, and final composition. It also reduces the common problem where one contractor solves only their own piece and leaves the exterior feeling disconnected.

That is why driveway, patio, turf, and wall work sit alongside fence services on this site. The end result is often better when the scope is coordinated from the start.
