Empire Fence installs **wrought iron fencing** for projects where curb appeal and security need to work together.

This is one of the clearest premium materials in the service mix. It adds definition to the frontage, frames entry points, and makes the property feel more intentional from the street.

## Typical uses

- Front-yard fencing
- Decorative boundary lines
- Gate and entry sequences
- Pool and security enclosures

## Design emphasis

Wrought iron is a visual material. The quality comes from proportion, spacing, alignment, and how the fence connects to gates, corners, and the overall property line.

## Best fit

Choose wrought iron when the perimeter needs to do more than close off space. It should add presence.
