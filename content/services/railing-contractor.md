Empire Fence works as a **railing contractor** for projects that need a stronger safety edge without giving up the look of the exterior.

Railings are often the detail that finishes a walkway, step run, patio edge, or elevated section properly. This service is for spaces that need both function and visual consistency.

## Typical railing projects

- Entry steps and walkways
- Patio and exterior edge detailing
- Elevated sections that need added safety
- Exterior upgrades that need a more finished boundary line

## Why it belongs in the lineup

Railing work fits naturally beside fencing and gate projects because it helps complete the same exterior perimeter language in a more resolved way.
