Empire Fence provides **turf installation** for clients who want a more controlled landscape finish without the maintenance demands of traditional grass.

This service works especially well when a fence, patio, or backyard project needs a final surface that stays visually clean year-round.

## Typical turf projects

- Backyard refreshes
- Patio-adjacent yard upgrades
- Family and pet-friendly landscape zones
- Low-maintenance exterior packages

## Why it matters here

Turf is not treated as a separate business. It is positioned as part of a broader outdoor transformation that makes the entire project feel complete.
