Empire Fence also handles **driveway and patio projects** for clients who want the exterior to feel solved as one coordinated scope.

Fence work often changes how the rest of the property should read. This service exists for the situations where the driveway, patio, or hardscape needs to be upgraded alongside the perimeter.

## Best use cases

- Entry sequences that need a cleaner arrival
- Backyard upgrades tied to a new fence line
- Exterior refreshes where hardscape and perimeter work should happen together

## The advantage

One team can shape the edge of the property and the adjacent outdoor surfaces in a way that feels more coherent when the work is done.
