Empire Fence builds **cinder block walls** for projects that need more structural presence than a light fence line can provide.

This is the heavier-duty perimeter option on the site. It fits privacy walls, defined property edges, and situations where long-term durability matters most.

## Common applications

- Backyard privacy walls
- Stronger side-yard boundaries
- Supportive landscape separation
- Perimeter lines that need more permanence

## Why clients choose block walls

They offer a more solid visual and structural edge. When the property needs a stronger line, a cinder block wall often becomes the right answer.
