Empire Fence provides **chain link fence installation** for projects where function, speed, and coverage matter first.

This is the straightforward perimeter option in the lineup. It is commonly chosen for practical site control, boundary definition, and larger coverage areas where budget and speed both matter.

## Common chain link projects

- Lot boundaries
- Utility edges
- Commercial perimeter work
- Yard enclosure
- Security-focused sections

## Why it stays in demand

Chain link is efficient. It covers space quickly and keeps the project focused on access, enclosure, and security without overcomplicating the material decision.
