Empire Fence builds **custom fences** for properties that need something more specific than a standard template.

Some projects need a certain material mix. Others need a layout that follows the site more carefully or an appearance that works with the architecture. This service is for those cases.

## When custom scope makes sense

- The property has unusual dimensions
- A standard privacy fence feels too generic
- The frontage needs a stronger visual identity
- The fence needs to coordinate with gates, walls, or hardscape

## The design approach

The process starts with the property and the goal. From there, Empire Fence can shape the layout, material choice, and finish language so the result feels intentional rather than improvised.
