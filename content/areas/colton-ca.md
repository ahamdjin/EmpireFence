## Fence Types We Install in Colton

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a strong choice for property owners who want a clean, neat appearance with lower maintenance. It works well for privacy fencing, side-yard separation, backyard enclosures, and homes where long-term ease of upkeep matters. For many Colton customers, vinyl offers a practical balance of appearance, privacy, and lasting value. It is especially appealing when a polished look matters just as much as day-to-day convenience.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing offers strength, visibility, and decorative appeal in one solution. It is a great fit for front-facing sections of a property, entry areas, and locations where security matters but a closed-in look is not the goal. This type of fencing works especially well when a property needs a stronger visual presence and a more refined exterior finish.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains one of the most requested options for backyard privacy and traditional curb appeal. It is well suited for shared property lines, side-yard enclosures, and homes where a warmer, more natural look is preferred. Wood is also a flexible option for customers who want a fence that can be tailored more closely to the layout and style of the property.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is durable, efficient, and practical for many residential and commercial applications. It is commonly used for utility areas, larger perimeters, commercial lots, side yards, and spaces where visibility and straightforward security are more important than decorative design. For customers who need a dependable boundary without overcomplicating the project, chain link is often the right answer.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers long-term durability, strong perimeter definition, and a solid visual presence. It works well for customers who want a fence built for performance while still maintaining a clean, professional appearance. Depending on the design, metal fencing can support both security-focused and style-focused goals, making it a versatile option for homes and businesses alike.
