## Fence Types We Install in Ontario

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a strong choice for Ontario property owners who want a clean, low-maintenance solution. It works especially well for privacy fencing, backyard enclosures, side-yard separation, and homes that benefit from a neat, polished look.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing offers visibility, structure, and decorative appeal at the same time. It is a great fit for front boundaries, entry areas, and properties that want security without closing off the view.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains one of the most popular options for privacy and curb appeal. It works well for backyard fencing, shared property lines, and residential spaces where a natural, classic look is the right fit.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is practical, durable, and efficient for many residential and commercial needs. It is often the right option for larger perimeters, utility areas, storage spaces, and straightforward security work.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers strength, stability, and a solid visual boundary. It is well suited for customers who want long-term performance and a fence that feels substantial in both residential and commercial settings.
