## Fence Types We Install in Upland

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a strong choice for Upland property owners who want a clean, attractive look with lower maintenance. It works especially well for privacy fencing, backyard enclosures, side-yard separation, and residential properties where consistency of appearance matters. For customers who want a fence that stays sharp with less ongoing upkeep, vinyl is often one of the most practical and visually appealing options.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing is ideal when a property needs strength and visibility with a more decorative appearance. It is often chosen for front-facing sections of a home, entry areas, driveways, and properties where presentation matters just as much as perimeter definition. This style is especially effective for customers who want a fence that adds architectural character while still providing structure and security.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains one of the most versatile options for privacy, warmth, and a more classic residential look. It works well for backyard boundaries, shared property lines, side-yard enclosures, and homes where a natural appearance fits the setting better than a more modern material. For many Upland properties, wood offers the right balance of function, flexibility, and visual comfort.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is a durable, efficient solution for customers who need visibility, containment, and practical perimeter control. It is often the right fit for utility areas, larger boundaries, side yards, service spaces, and commercial properties where function is the top priority. When the goal is straightforward security and dependable performance, chain link remains one of the most practical choices available.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers long-term strength, solid perimeter definition, and a strong visual presence. It is a dependable option for properties that need a fence built for durability while still maintaining a clean and professional appearance. Depending on the design, metal fencing can support both decorative goals and security-focused needs, making it a strong fit for a wide range of residential and commercial projects.
