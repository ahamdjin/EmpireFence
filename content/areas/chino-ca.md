## Fence Types We Install in Chino

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a popular option for customers who want privacy and a clean appearance with lower maintenance. It is a strong fit for backyard enclosures, side-yard separation, and homes where a tidy, uniform look matters.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing offers a combination of strength, openness, and decorative appeal. It works well for entry areas, front-facing boundaries, and properties that want security while maintaining visibility.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains a favorite for privacy, warmth, and flexibility in design. It is well suited for backyard fencing, shared property lines, and custom layouts where a natural look is preferred.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is durable, efficient, and practical for a wide range of residential and commercial uses. It is commonly chosen for larger boundaries, utility spaces, side yards, and sites that need straightforward security.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers long-term strength and a more substantial look. It is a smart choice for properties that need a secure, durable boundary with a clean and professional appearance.
