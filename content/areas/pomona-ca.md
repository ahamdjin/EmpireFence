## Fence Types We Install in Pomona

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a strong choice for Pomona property owners who want a clean appearance with lower maintenance. It works especially well for privacy fencing, backyard enclosures, and side-yard separation where a polished finish and easier upkeep both matter. For customers who want a fence that stays neat without requiring the same level of ongoing maintenance as some other materials, vinyl is often a practical long-term option.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing is ideal when a property needs structure, visibility, and a more decorative appearance at the same time. It works especially well in front-facing sections, entry areas, and properties that benefit from a stronger architectural presence. This type of fencing is a strong fit when the goal is to define the property clearly without making it feel overly closed in.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains one of the most requested choices for backyard privacy and a more traditional residential look. It is well suited for shared property lines, side-yard enclosures, and homes where a natural appearance fits the setting better than a more modern material. For many Pomona properties, wood offers the right balance of privacy, warmth, and design flexibility.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is durable, efficient, and practical for customers who need visibility, containment, and dependable perimeter control. It is commonly used for utility spaces, larger boundaries, side yards, storage areas, and commercial properties where straightforward function matters most. When the priority is secure enclosure without unnecessary complexity, chain link is often the smartest and most efficient solution.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers long-term strength, solid perimeter definition, and a substantial finished look. It is a dependable option for properties that need durability while still maintaining a clean and professional appearance. Depending on the design, metal fencing can support both security-focused and style-driven goals, making it a strong fit for a wide range of residential and commercial projects in Pomona.
