## Fence Types We Install in Fontana

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a practical choice for Fontana property owners who want privacy and a clean appearance with lower maintenance. It works well for many residential applications where durability and ease of care matter.

Wrought iron fencing is a strong option for front boundaries, entry areas, and properties that want a more decorative look without giving up structure and security.

Wood fencing remains a versatile option for backyard privacy, side-yard separation, and a more traditional residential appearance. It works especially well where a natural look is preferred.
