## Fence Types We Install in Redlands

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a strong choice for Redlands property owners who want privacy and a clean appearance with lower maintenance. It works especially well for backyard fencing, side-yard separation, and homes where long-term appearance matters just as much as everyday convenience. For customers who want a fence that stays neat and polished with less upkeep, vinyl is often one of the smartest long-term options.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing is ideal when a property needs structure, visibility, and a more decorative appearance at the same time. It is a strong fit for front-facing sections, entry areas, and properties that benefit from a more refined architectural look. This style works especially well when the goal is to frame the property clearly without making it feel overly enclosed.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains one of the most requested choices for privacy and a more traditional residential look. It is well suited for backyard boundaries, shared property lines, and homes where a natural appearance fits the setting. For many Redlands properties, wood offers the right balance of privacy, warmth, and design flexibility, especially when the goal is to keep the property looking established and welcoming.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is durable, efficient, and practical for customers who need dependable perimeter control, visibility, and straightforward enclosure. It is commonly used for service areas, utility spaces, side boundaries, and commercial properties where function matters most. When the priority is secure enclosure without unnecessary complexity, chain link is often the most efficient option.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers long-term strength, strong perimeter definition, and a substantial finished look. It is a dependable choice for properties that need durability while still maintaining a clean and professional appearance. Depending on the design, metal fencing can support both security-focused and style-driven goals, making it a strong fit for a wide range of Redlands projects.
