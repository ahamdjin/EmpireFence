## Fence Types We Install in Claremont

### Vinyl Fence

Vinyl fencing is a strong choice for Claremont property owners who want a clean, consistent appearance with lower maintenance. It works especially well for privacy fencing, backyard enclosures, and side-yard separation where a polished finish and everyday convenience both matter. For customers who want a fence that stays neat with less ongoing upkeep, vinyl can be one of the most practical options available.

### Wrought Iron Fence

Wrought iron fencing is ideal when a property needs structure and security while maintaining openness and architectural character. It works especially well in front-facing areas, entry zones, and properties that benefit from a more refined look. This type of fence is often the right answer when visibility matters but the property still needs a stronger visual frame.

### Wood Fence

Wood fencing remains one of the most versatile options for privacy, warmth, and a more traditional residential feel. It is well suited for backyard boundaries, shared property lines, side-yard enclosures, and homes where a natural look is the better fit. For many Claremont properties, wood offers the right balance of privacy, familiarity, and design flexibility.

### Chain Link Fence

Chain link fencing is a practical choice for customers who need visibility, durability, and straightforward perimeter control. It is commonly used for utility spaces, larger boundaries, side yards, service areas, and commercial properties where function comes first. When the priority is dependable enclosure without unnecessary complexity, chain link is often the most efficient solution.

### Metal Fence

Metal fencing offers long-term durability, strong boundary definition, and a substantial finished look. It is a dependable option for properties that need a fence built for performance while still supporting a clean and professional appearance. Depending on the design, metal fencing can work well for both decorative and security-focused applications, making it a versatile fit for a wide range of Claremont properties.
