## Who We Are

At Empire Fence Inc, we are a dedicated fence contractor serving homeowners, businesses, and property managers with high-quality fencing solutions built for security, privacy, and curb appeal.

We specialize in designing and installing fences that are made to last, using dependable materials, skilled workmanship, and a customer-first approach. Whether the project is a residential backyard fence, a commercial perimeter, decorative metal fencing, gates, or a custom solution, our team is committed to delivering results that combine function, durability, and style.

## Why We Started

We started Empire Fence Inc with a simple goal: to give customers a better experience when hiring a fence contractor.

Too many property owners were dealing with poor communication, rushed workmanship, weak materials, and projects that did not hold up over time. We believed customers deserved better.

Our company was built on the idea that every fence should be installed with care, every customer should be treated with honesty, and every project should reflect the pride we take in our work.

## Meet the Team

Behind every project is a team that brings experience, attention to detail, and a strong commitment to quality.

Our company is made up of knowledgeable estimators, project coordinators, skilled installers, and team leaders who understand every stage of the fencing process. From the first consultation to the final walkthrough, our team works together to make sure every project is completed smoothly, professionally, and to a high standard.

We believe a great fence is not just about materials. It is also about the people building it. That is why we invest in experienced professionals who value workmanship, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

## Awards and Credentials

We take pride in maintaining professional standards that customers can trust.

Our team is committed to ongoing training, proven installation practices, and staying current with local requirements, safety expectations, and product knowledge. We are proud to be licensed and insured, and we work hard to uphold the standards expected of a reputable fence contractor.

Over the years, Empire Fence Inc has built recognition through dependable results, customer trust, and a reputation for quality service across residential and commercial fencing work.

## What Makes Us Different

What sets Empire Fence Inc apart is our combination of craftsmanship, customer service, and attention to detail.

We do not believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every property is different, and every customer has different priorities. That is why we take the time to understand the purpose of the project, whether it is privacy, security, safety, appearance, or long-term durability.

Our team focuses on quality materials, precise installation, and a smooth customer experience from start to finish. We also understand the importance of clean job sites, proper planning, and getting the details right so the finished fence looks great and performs exactly as it should.
